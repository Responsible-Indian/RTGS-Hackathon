Systemic Friction Points

Anatomy of Delay

Cardiac emergencies demand seamless coordination, but systemic fragmentation transforms the golden hour into waiting periods.

01

Sequential Dependency Trap

Each step waits for the previous. No parallelization—every delay compounds into the next.

02

Fragmented Data Silos

Systems don't speak to each other. Patient data in ambulance doesn't flow to hospital dashboards.

03

Signal Integrity

Motion artifacts and lead placement errors in moving ambulances force manual re-capture delays.

The Compounding Effect

No single bottleneck operates in isolation. Together, they transform what should be a streamlined response into a fragmented series of waiting periods.
