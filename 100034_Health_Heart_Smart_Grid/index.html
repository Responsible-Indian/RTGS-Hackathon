Systemic Friction Points

Anatomy of Delay

Cardiac emergencies demand seamless coordination, but systemic fragmentation transforms the golden hour into a series of preventable waiting periods.

01

Sequential Dependency Trap

Each step waits for the previous one. ECG capture must finish before interpretation; interpretation before call. No parallelization.

02

Fragmented Data Silos

Ambulances, PHCs, and hospitals operate as isolated islands. Patient data doesn't flow to hospital dashboards automatically.

03

Signal Integrity

Patient motion and poor connectivity degrade ECG quality. Without AI filtering, paramedics must re-capture signals frequently.

The Solution

The Intelligence Layer

AI weaves a unified response fabric—connecting ambulances, clinicians, and hospitals into a single, synchronized network.
