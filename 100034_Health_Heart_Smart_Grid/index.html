Systemic Friction Points

Anatomy of Delay

Cardiac emergencies demand seamless coordination, but systemic fragmentation transforms the golden hour into a series of preventable waiting periods.

Sequential Dependency Trap

Each step waits for the previous step. ECG capture must finish before interpretation. No parallelization—every delay compounds.

Fragmented Data Silos

Patient data captured in the ambulance doesn't flow to hospital dashboards. Systems don't speak to each other, requiring manual re-entry.

Rural Connectivity Void

Significant portions of AP have intermittent connectivity. Without offline caching, data is lost in transit.

Blind Arrival Syndrome

Hospitals receive no advance warning. Cath labs remain unprepared until the ambulance arrives at the door.

These bottlenecks compound into failure
The Solution

The Intelligence Layer

Where fragmented systems once created delays, AI now weaves a unified response fabric—connecting ambulances, clinicians, and hospitals.

Edge AI Diagnosis

Instant STEMI detection on ambulance tablets. Clinical-grade accuracy in under 5 seconds, even offline.

Unified Data Fabric

Standard adapters normalize ECG ingestion. Data captured once flows everywhere instantly via secure APIs.

Smart Pre-Alerting

Cath labs prepared before ambulance arrives. Routes to nearest PCI-capable facility based on real-time availability.

Offline-First Design

Store-and-forward via MQTT ensures zero data loss. Diagnosis continues locally; data syncs when signal returns.
