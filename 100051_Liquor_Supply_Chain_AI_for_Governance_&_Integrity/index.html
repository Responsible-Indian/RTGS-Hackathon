Root Cause Analysis: AP Liquor Chain

Where the Pipeline Constricts

The governance gaps persist not from lack of effort, but from structural constraints embedded in how systems, processes, and data flows are architected. These root causes explain why the fractures won't heal on their own.

01 / 06

Fragmented Legacy Systems

Permit issuance, production logging, transport GPS tracking, and retail licensing operate on separate, incompatible systems built across different technology eras. No master data layer exists to correlate records.

Result

Cross-referencing a single shipment requires manual queries across multiple systemsif the data exists at all.

Feeds: Broken Paper Trail, Blind Spots in Material Movement
02 / 06

Transformation Reconciliation Gaps

Molasses transforms to ENA, ENA to spiriteach with different units, batch splits, timing delays, and conversion yields. No system normalizes these transformations to enable true mass-balance verification across production stages.

Result

False mismatches flood manual reviews while genuine diversions hide in legitimate-looking conversion variance.

Feeds: Blind Spots in Material Movement, Broken Paper Trail
03 / 06

Manual Handoff Cascade

Every transition between permit issuance, production, dispatch, transport, and retail requires manual data entry and human verification. Each handoff introduces delay, transcription errors, and data loss opportunities.

Result

By the time goods reach retail, cumulative errors make end-to-end reconciliation unreliable or impossible.

Feeds: Broken Paper Trail, Counterfeit Infiltration
04 / 06

Real-Time Data & GPS Integrity Vacuum

No unified stream processing correlates GPS data, permits, and production logs in real time. Data is batch-processed hours or days after events. GPS signal dropouts and spoofing go undetected with no integrity verification.

Result

By the time anomalies surface in reports, diverted goods have reached illicit markets and route evidence is gone.

Feeds: Ghost Routes & Silent Diversions, Porous Border Monitoring
05 / 06

Jurisdictional Fragmentation

No inter-state data sharing APIs exist between AP and neighboring states. Different geofence definitions, incompatible GPS vendor systems, and zero visibility into vehicles once they cross borders create systematic blind spots.

Result

Cross-border smuggling exploits price differentials with near-zero detection risk at jurisdictional boundaries.

Feeds: Porous Border Monitoring, Ghost Routes & Silent Diversions
06 / 06

Label Trust & Verification Gap

No centralized digital label registry exists. Authentic labels vary due to print drift and field damage. Manual verification cannot reliably distinguish damaged-but-genuine labels from counterfeitsespecially offline in variable field conditions.

Result

Counterfeit products pass visual inspection while legitimate but damaged labels trigger false rejections.

Feeds: Counterfeit Infiltration
